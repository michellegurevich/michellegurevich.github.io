Welcome to my personal webpage. I am Michelle Gurevich and I study gravitational waves at Imperial College London. Future research proposal: SKItTLES (Source Kinetic Imaging to Translate Exotic Signatures)

Modified dispersion relations for gravitational waves

I am a current MSc student working with Carlo Contaldi in the Theoretical Physics Group at Imperial College London, and am in the process of applying to PhD programs for fall of 2022. I see a career in science as a unique opportunity to make a difference with work that is meaningful as well as impactful.

Originally from New York, I made my way to London by way of the American midwest, where I studied at the University of Michigan for my bachelor's in mathematics. You can find a bit more on my studies and work in my CV, and reach out directly via email. Please report any and all typos!
